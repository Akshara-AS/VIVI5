    Look into the brighter side

    Goals are something you're acting on. Dreams are something you create in your mind that can take any shape or form, while goals are based on taking action. With goals, a lot of focus and attention is needed. Dreams, on the other hand, don't need that kind of focus.But see a dream without a goal is a wish and a goal without a plan is just a dream. Life goals are essentially everything you want to do in life . Setting goals gives you purpose, and they help you to guide your life. Think of life goals as your North Star and dream to outshine it :)
